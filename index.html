Philosophy

Three Rules. Zero Compromise.

Every recommendation in HaloFinance follows the same three-word framework.

⚡
Principle 01
Easier

Fewer steps, less friction. The path of least resistance to the best outcome. If two options produce the same result, the simpler one wins — every time.

🔥
Principle 02
Faster

Quick execution beats perfect optimization. Done beats perfect every time. A plan you execute this week is worth more than a perfect plan you start next year.

💵
Principle 03
Biggest Return

Every recommendation leads with the option that puts the most money in your pocket. Maximum impact, minimum wasted motion.

How It Works

Set up in five minutes flat.

No accounts, no subscriptions, no waiting. Fork it, load it, use it.

→
01
Fork the Repo

Clone HaloFinance into a new private repo on your GitHub account. Keep it private — your numbers stay yours.

→
02
Load Claude

Open a Claude Project and upload all files from the knowledge/ folder. Claude now has full financial context.

→
03
Add Your Numbers

Fill in the personal/ templates with your real income, debts, and goals. Upload those too.

04
Ask Anything

Taxes, debt payoff order, retirement contributions, W-4 adjustments, budgeting — Claude now answers with your context, not generic advice.

Who It's For
