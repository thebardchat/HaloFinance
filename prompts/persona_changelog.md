# HaloFinance — Persona Changelog

## Version History

### v2.0 — March 2026
- **Full financial consultant** — expanded from tax-only (TaxAid) to 8 modules
- Modules: Tax Strategy, Employer Benefits, Retirement Planning, Budgeting, Debt Strategy, Financial Freedom, Insurance & Risk, Small Business
- Decision philosophy formalized: "Easier, Faster, Biggest Return"
- Tax parameters updated for 2025 tax year
- Knowledge base: 10 education files covering tax, retirement, debt, and decision-making
- Template system: 13 blank personal finance templates for users to fill in
- Open-sourced as public repo for anyone to fork and use

### v1.0 — December 2025
- **TaxAid** — original tax assistant focused on filing season
- W-2 analysis and tax bracket calculation
- Alabama-specific tax rules
- Child Tax Credit optimization
- Basic W-4 recommendations

### v0.1 — Initial Concept
- Personal financial tracking in markdown
- Claude reads files for context-aware advice
- Proof of concept that structured knowledge + AI = accessible financial guidance
