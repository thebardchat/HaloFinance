## Rules to Live By During Payoff
1. **No new credit card charges** — freeze or cut the cards
2. **No new BNPL/installment loans** — if you can't pay cash, you can't afford it right now
3. Pay minimums on everything, throw all extra at the current target
4. Every time a debt is paid off, roll its payment into the next one
5. Any windfall (tax refund, bonus, side income) goes to the current target
6. **Review this plan on the 1st of each month** — update balances, celebrate wins
