## Decision Filter: Easier, Faster, Biggest Return
Every financial decision runs through this filter:
- **Biggest return** — which option puts the most money in our pocket?
- **Easier** — which option has fewer steps and less friction?
- **Faster** — which option gets results sooner?
- If the "cheap" option costs more in lost returns, it's not actually cheaper
- Stick with what's working unless the alternative clearly wins on all three
