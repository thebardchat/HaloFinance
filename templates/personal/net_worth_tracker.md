## Monthly Check-In Routine (1st of each month)
1. Log into bank — write down checking + savings balances
2. Log into 401(k) provider — write down balance
3. Check credit card statements — write down balances owed
4. Check loan balances (mortgage portal, auto loan portal)
5. Update this sheet
6. Calculate net worth
7. Compare to last month — are you going up or down?

## The Rule of 72
Your money doubles every (72 / return rate) years.
- At 7% (stock market average): doubles every ~10 years
- $50K at age 35 → $100K at 45 → $200K at 55 → $400K at 65
- That's why starting NOW matters more than the amount
