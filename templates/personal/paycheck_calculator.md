## How to Calculate
1. **Gross** = (Regular hrs x rate) + (OT hrs x rate x 1.5)
2. **Federal tax** = (Gross - 401k - insurance) x marginal rate, minus credits/payperiod
3. **FICA** = Gross x 7.65% (Social Security 6.2% + Medicare 1.45%)
4. **401(k)** = Your per-paycheck contribution (pre-tax)
5. **Insurance** = Your per-paycheck health/dental/vision premium
6. **Take-home** = Gross - Federal - FICA - State - 401k - Insurance
