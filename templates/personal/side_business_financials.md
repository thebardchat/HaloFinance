## Tax Implications When Revenue Starts
- Report on Schedule C (Form 1040)
- Self-employment tax: 15.3% on net profit (in addition to income tax)
- Quarterly estimated taxes required (Form 1040-ES)
- Due dates: April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15
- Home office deduction: simplified $5/sq ft up to 300 sq ft ($1,500 max) OR actual method

## Entity Evolution Plan
- $0-$40K net profit: Stay sole proprietor
- $40K-$80K net profit: Consider LLC (liability protection)
- $80K+ net profit: Evaluate S-Corp election (save on self-employment tax)
